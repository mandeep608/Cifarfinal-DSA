CIFAR10CNN is a Convolutional Neural Network trained on the CIFAR10 dataset.
CIFAR10CNN is trained to recognize the difference between the 10 categories which are airplane, car/automobile, bird, cat, deer, dog, frog, horse, ship and semitruck.
